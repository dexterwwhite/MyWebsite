Hello! My name is Dexter White and I am a junior computer science major currently attending Texas A&M university! If I had to describe myself in only three words, I would definitely choose the adjectives: positive, driven, and comedic.


With the introduction of Covid into our lives a year and a half ago, life changed drastically. It brought about many negative emotions and it would seem like there was a constant negative haze surrounding every aspect of life. Surprisingly, this led to me entering a new, very positive phase of my life. I began eating healthier meals, I tried to get the correct amount of sleep each night, I began lifting weights and running 2-3 times a week, and I began to implement aspects of stoicism into my life.


After about a year and a half, I have never felt better! Most days I wake up very excited to take on the day and push myself as hard as I can. My past view of the world has completely changed, and I am constantly admiring how much my outlook has improved since then. I also try to spread my happiness with others as much as possible, whether it is through supporting them in their endeavors or attempting to make them laugh with cheesy jokes (I view them as funny, though, which is the most important part!).


I don't mean to come off as conceited, but I am definitely someone I would like to have as a team member. Having dealt with very bad teammates on numerous occasions, I try my best to act opposite to these poor teammates. It is always my goal to be accountable and hardworking, so when I am delegated work it becomes my mission to complete it to the best of my ability. I am also very agreeable, so you should not have to worry about getting into an argument with me over the logistics of a project. As a positive, agreeable, and hardworking person, I believe I would fit very well into any team.
